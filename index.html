About Me

I've recently switched careers, again, having been a business owner in Richmond for the last 6 years. Before that, I was a manager for a Parks & Rec organization in Northern Virginia. Having sold my business, I am now taking off on a new adventure: coding.

I am currently in an intensive program at the University of Richmond learning full-stack coding. Once I ace the program, I'll be able to handle all aspects of web development. In addition to my quickly-increasing coding skills, I also have a wealth of business experience, including managing teams of people, customer service, marketing, facilities management, budgeting and accounting, and many other executive functions.

I am married to a wonderful wife, and have three children, ages 13, 9 and 8; they're all girls. I enjoy outdoor activities such as rock climbing, backpacking, and skiing, as well as reading and spending time with my family.
